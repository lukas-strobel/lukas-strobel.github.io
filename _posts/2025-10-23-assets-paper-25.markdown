How can AI help making lecture videos accessible for student who are blind or have low vision?

In Katharina Anderer's paper, we investigated this questions and interviewed these students to develop a prototype that can help them navigate lecture material with a AI-powered chatbot.

You can read the paper [here](https://doi.org/10.1145/3663547.3746349).
